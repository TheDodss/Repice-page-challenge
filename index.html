Omelette Recipe

A quick and tasty meal, perfect for any time of the day.

Start Cooking

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional: cheese, veggies, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs, cook until edges are set, then add fillings.
  4. Fold carefully, cook for another minute, and serve hot.
